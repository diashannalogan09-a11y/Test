Our Story

Built from purpose. Rooted in healing.

Logan Pathway to Hope & Healing was created from a calling to provide care that feels intentional, structured, and deeply human.

Start the Pathway

More than services.

This is a pathway for individuals and families who need support, stability, compassion, and a place where healing can begin with dignity.

Where compassion meets structure, and healing becomes possible.

Logan Pathway was built from the belief that people deserve more than temporary solutions. They deserve real support, real connection, and real opportunities to heal.

Our work is rooted in compassion, structure, and hope. We understand that many individuals and families come to services carrying trauma, stress, broken trust, and uncertainty. Our goal is to meet them with care, help them stabilize, and walk with them toward growth.

Whether through outpatient therapy, group counseling, assessments, residential support, or future program development, Logan Pathway exists to help people move from surviving to healing, and from healing to purpose.

Healing today. Hope for tomorrow. Purpose for life.

Leadership

The heart behind the pathway.

Founder & Therapist

Diashanna Logan

Diashanna Logan is the founder and therapist behind Logan Pathway to Hope & Healing. Her work is grounded in trauma-informed care, emotional wellness, crisis intervention, and helping individuals build the skills needed to manage life’s challenges.

She specializes in supporting individuals navigating anxiety, depression, trauma, family conflict, emotional regulation, life transitions, and stress. Her approach is structured, compassionate, honest, and empowering.

Diashanna believes therapy should be a space where people feel safe enough to tell the truth, supported enough to heal, and encouraged enough to grow.

Co-Founder & Chief Impact Officer

Amber Jones-Logan

Amber Jones-Logan serves as Co-Founder and Chief Impact Officer, helping shape the heart, direction, and community impact of Logan Pathway to Hope & Healing.

Her role centers on ensuring the mission reaches people in meaningful, measurable, and lasting ways. Amber’s leadership strengthens the organization’s focus on connection, access, advocacy, and positive change.

As Chief Impact Officer, Amber helps ensure every service, partnership, and future program reflects the heart of the mission: helping people feel seen, supported, and connected to a pathway forward.
