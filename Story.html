OUR STORY

Built from purpose. Rooted in healing.

Logan Pathway to Hope & Healing was created to provide care that feels intentional, structured, and deeply human.

More than services. This is a pathway.

Logan Pathway was built from the belief that individuals deserve more than temporary solutions. They deserve real support, real connection, and real opportunities to heal.

We exist to help individuals and families move from surviving to healing—and from healing to purpose.

Healing today. Hope for tomorrow. Purpose for life.

FOUNDER & THERAPIST

Diashanna Logan

Diashanna Logan is the founder and visionary behind Logan Pathway. As a therapist, she specializes in trauma-informed care, emotional regulation, anxiety, depression, family conflict, and life transitions.

Her approach is structured, compassionate, and focused on helping individuals develop real tools for growth and healing.

She believes therapy should be a safe space where people can be honest, supported, and empowered to change.

CO-FOUNDER & CHIEF IMPACT OFFICER

Amber Jones-Logan

Amber ensures that the mission of Logan Pathway reaches people in meaningful ways. Her focus is on community impact, connection, and expanding access to care.

She plays a key role in shaping how the organization supports individuals, families, and partners.

Her work ensures that every service creates real, lasting change.
